Overview
In the personal care industry, businesses face challenges in effectively integrating and promoting add-on services within their service offerings. Currently, there is a lack of a streamlined system that allows for both centralized management of these services across multiple locations and personalized customer experiences during the booking process. This gap results in missed opportunities for upselling and cross-selling, ultimately impacting the overall revenue and customer satisfaction.
Solution Summary
Boulevard Labs’ Booking Agent is a sophisticated scheduling and booking application designed for the personal care industry. The application enables businesses to efficiently manage and integrate add-on services to base services, enhancing the customer booking experience and increasing business revenue through upselling and cross-selling opportunities.
Key Features
Business-Level Configuration and Customization:
Businesses can configure and define add-on services via Centralized Add-On Management UI, ensuring consistency across all locations.
Individual locations can override and customize their add-on services, tailoring offerings to local market needs and preferences.
Consumer-Level Online Booking Enhancements:
During the online booking process, customers are presented with relevant add-on services that complement their primary service selection, such as suggesting a blowout when booking a haircut.
Business-Level Booking Flow Improvements:
The application supports front desk staff by suggesting potential cross-sell and upsell opportunities during the booking and check-in processes, ensuring that every customer interaction is maximized for revenue potential.
Phase 1 Approach
Initially, Boulevard Online Booking will utilize a heuristic approach to recommend add-on services based on popular combinations and staff input. As the application gathers more data on customer preferences and behaviors, we plan to deploy machine learning models that will enable smarter, personalized recommendations, enhancing the upselling process and further tailoring the customer experience.
